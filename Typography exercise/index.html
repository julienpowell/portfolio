Dear urban citizens,

What do you expect of your online relationship with local government? With daily life ever busier and more complex, you crave immediacy, ease, and satisfaction. The Googles, Amazons, Facebooks. 

Hardly surprising, city websites don’t measure up. They're old, huge, slow, intricate beasts, just like their organizations. Nonetheless, your expectations rise faster than they are met.

When you interact with government, it’s undermining to hit a brick wall of “who does what?” before you can even participate, be heard, get information, ask questions, complete transactions.
